# Glossary

- **UTXO**: Unspent Transaction Output, the spendable coin unit in Bitcoin.
- **Covenant (contextual usage)**: Script constraints that restrict future spending conditions.
- **Timelock**: Script-enforced delay before spend validity.
- **CLTV**: `OP_CHECKLOCKTIMEVERIFY`, absolute timelock condition.
- **CSV**: `OP_CHECKSEQUENCEVERIFY`, relative timelock condition.
- **Commitment device**: Mechanism that makes future deviation costly.
- **Repeated game**: Strategic game played across multiple rounds.
- **Discount factor (`δ`)**: Weight on future payoffs.
- **Defection**: Action that maximizes short-term gain at cooperation partner's expense.
- **Reciprocity**: Conditional cooperation based on observed past behavior.
- **ESS**: Evolutionarily Stable Strategy, robust against invasion by alternatives.
- **Lock horizon (`H`)**: Duration of capital commitment.
- **Lock ratio (`λ`)**: Fraction of capital committed to constraints.
- **DCR**: Defection Cost Ratio.
- **LDI**: Lock Duration Index.
- **RCS**: Reciprocity Consistency Score.
