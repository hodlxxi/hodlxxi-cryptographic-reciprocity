# Volume II: Human Nature

## Purpose

This volume studies the human actor that HODLXXI must actually serve and constrain.

A technically correct system that misunderstands human nature will fail.

## Initial themes

- Status
- Fear
- Cooperation
- Gratitude
- Debt
- Family
- Inheritance
- Honor
- Loyalty
- Resentment
- Envy
- Sacrifice
- Meaning

## Working premise

Humans are not only utility maximizers. They are bounded, emotional, narrative-driven, status-sensitive, loyal, resentful, grateful, capable of sacrifice, and capable of betrayal.

## Falsification focus

This volume fails wherever its model of the human actor causes HODLXXI to reward symbolic performance instead of durable commitment.
